Frequently Asked Questions

What services do relief centers provide?

Relief centers provide essential services such as food, water, medical aid, and temporary shelter during disasters.

How can I find the nearest relief center?

You can use our platform to locate the nearest relief center based on your location.

Can I volunteer at a relief center?

Yes, many relief centers welcome volunteers. Contact your nearest center for more information.
