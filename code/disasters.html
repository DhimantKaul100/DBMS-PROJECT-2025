Frequently Asked Questions

How is disaster information used?

Disaster information is used to coordinate rescue efforts, allocate resources, and inform the public about safety measures.

Can I report a disaster?

Yes, you can report a disaster by filling out the disaster form and providing accurate details.

How is disaster severity determined?

Disaster severity is determined based on factors such as impact, scale, and the number of people affected.
