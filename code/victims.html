Frequently Asked Questions

How is victim information used?

Victim information is used to coordinate rescue efforts, provide medical aid, and reunite families.

Can I update victim details?

Yes, victim details can be updated by authorized personnel to ensure accurate records.

How can I report a missing person?

You can report a missing person by filling out the victim form and selecting "Missing" as the status.
