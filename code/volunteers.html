Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of volunteers?

Volunteers assist in disaster relief efforts, including rescue operations, medical aid, logistics, and providing support to victims.

How can I become a volunteer?

You can become a volunteer by filling out the volunteer form and providing your skills and availability.

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

No, prior experience is not mandatory. Training and guidance will be provided based on your role.
