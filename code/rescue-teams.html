Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of rescue teams?

Rescue teams are responsible for saving lives, providing emergency assistance, and ensuring the safety of disaster-affected individuals.

How can I contact a rescue team?

You can contact rescue teams through their listed contact numbers or by reaching out to local disaster management authorities.

Can I volunteer to join a rescue team?

Yes, many rescue teams welcome volunteers. Contact your nearest rescue team for more information.
